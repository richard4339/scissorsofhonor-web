3 Star First Attack

You are expected to use your first attack within few hours after war starts, and it has to be aimed for 3 Stars (For TH7, TH8, and most TH9)

No Looting Allowed

All attacks must be aimed toward "Star-gain" or "Experience." This means NO ONE is allowed to "TH Snipe" or "Barch 50%" for the sake of LOOTING. Unless you are told otherwise.

Use Clash Caller

Refer to Clash Caller before attacking! Claims do expire based upon how much time is left in the war.
