Preamble
  • Wars are started on Thursdays and Saturdays Prep day is day 1 and battle day is day 2
  • The first war search on Thursday is usually started at around 7:30AM CT, thats 6:30AM ET, 4:30AM PT, 11:30AM GMT, 4:00PM IST, 9:30PM AEST
    I cant be bothered to do the maths on all the time zones but you can work it out based on where you are :-P
  • The war on Saturday is usually started at some point after Jordan gets up. This is usually around 9:00AM CT.
  • Additionally once preparation day has started, we accept no new applications from members who people in the clan do not already know, to prevent spies from the opposing clan joining
  • As we are a nice clan, if an application comes in and the applicant is likely to be on-line, they could be accepted, welcomed and then given the explanation that as we are in a war, we will have to kick them but to re-apply after one / both wars are done?
Participation
  • If for whatever reason you don't think that you either want to, or will be able to participate then you can leave the clan at some point BEFORE clan war is started (Timings above) and reapply to join.
  • There is no penalty for doing this. You will be accepted back as soon as possible and re-instated to your previous level in the clan
  • The goal is for 100% participation in each war even if the war is smaller as a result. Everyone should aim to attack twice
  • If for some reason you don't think you will be able to participate in the war because something comes up, drop a message in clan chat and someone should read it before it drops off the bottom. This gives the clan a chance to re-organize and reallocate any bases that you may have claimed
Claiming
Use ClashCaller.com and read our War Rules page.
Clashcaller.com is a third-party website that we use to manage our attacks. Every member is entitled to 1 call, that is to reserve a base that they would like to hit (3 stars!) and is expected to execute an attack within the first 12 hours! After that 12 hour period, clashcaller no longer applies. Ask our elders/staff for target. DO NOT attack on your own without confirming your target with one of our elders/staff.
It's very simple, just click "Join War" and enter the Unique Code that will be provided by our staff at the beginning of Preparation Day. Always check Clan Mail on Preparation Day. Once you get the code, go to clashcaller.com and enter the code. You will be redirected to a new page. Add your name to the # that you wish to attack. For example: You decided to 3-star enemy's #10, add your name next to #10 in clashcaller so everyone knows that you are going to hit that base.
Clan Castle
  • Try and make your CC hard to get to and as centered as possible in your base. It can make any attack a lot trickier for the opponent. As an example, in the last war the one attack on my base had the attacker waste 1:30s (half the attack time) trying to get rid of my CC troops. In the end she sent in a dragon which was destroyed, after which only then did she start her main attack. Unsurprisingly she only managed a 1-star attack and ran out of time, nowhere near getting the TH
  • As for donations for defense. Its best left to the players with higher level troops. At the moment, Jeveland organizes this with the other higher players so please refrain from doing CC donations voluntarily
  • If you have particular requirements for your clan castle, then let whoever is doing the donations know in advance, or leave a message in your war CC request box
  • We don't ever want to see wall-breakers, giants or goblins in the CC. EVER! (Unless theres some sort of bizarre special request) Also, all elixir troops should be at least level 4
  • Always check your clan castle before war starts and if you find troops that you don't like, get replacement troops trained from another clan member before you dump whats already there. Better to have a full CC of underpowered troops than one with empty space because you didn't get troops in time!
Attacking
  • The aim is for as many of the lower players to get their CLAIMED attacks done within the first 12 hours of the war day. This way the lower bases can be cleaned up as soon as possible if any stars are missed
  • Always try to go into your attack with something in your CC. If you need something specific, request it in clan chat and someone should respond to let you know they are training it for you along with how long you will have to wait.
  • For players in the bottom 5, it might be cheap but you could request hogs in your attack CC. You'd be amazed at how much easier a base becomes to 3-star when the some / all of the splash damage defenses are gone ;-)
  • If you need ideas / advice / or constructive criticism for your proposed attack, feel free to ask in clan chat and hopefully someone will be there to give you advice
  • When doing cleanup, you should look at the bases you can 3-star and prioritize in the following order
  • bases already attacked with only one or zero stars b) bottom bases c) cover for someone else's cleanup attack if it fails d) 2 starred bases
  • If we get to the point where in all likelihood, the war is already won (Check with an elder on how likely the win is). Everyone who has an attack left should try a practice attack against an appropriate base. We get better with practice so try to take the opportunity to improve whenever we are presented with one You get 2 attacks, try and use them both!
    Note also that loot attacking is no longer allowed
  • We don't currently use scouting attacks. But if you ever wanted to help expose the CC and any outside traps / hidden teslas, my advice would be to spread a full army of barbarians, in a circle around the base. You'd be surprised by how much damage they could do!
  • If a replay exists for a previous attack on your base, make sure to watch it for the information on where the traps are / aren't, whats in the CC etc
  • ALWAYS GET RID OF THEIR CC TROOPS FIRST!, especially if you are planning on using giants. If you need help or advice on the easiest ways to lure and destroy them, ask in clan chat
